ClimateViewer 3D

ClimateViewer 3D's live updates on severe weather, earthquakes, fires, and other life-threatening events will give you peace of mind in one all-inclusive app. With cutting-edge technology, real-time situational awareness, and a visual tour of our planetary health risks, ClimateViewer 3D is your source for up-to-the-second live intel on a stunning 3D globe.

ClimateViewer 3D gives you real-time atmospheric and geophysical monitoring with maps covering geoengineering, weather modification, climate change, pollution, privacy, exploration, migration, geosciences, architecture, green energy solutions, sunken ships, airplane crash sites and more. Create your own mix of map layers and share your screen's current location, base layer, overlays, and markers! Please report any issues, stay safe, and have fun. Knowing is half the battle.

Instructions

Click the "View Layer" button () in the top left to show or hide the layer menu, then click on a map layer in the list to make it appear on the globe. Click the layer again to remove it from the globe. Click the folder icon next to any layer to show details about that layer. For more information, watch the YouTube tutorial video above.

Does Cesium require an install or a plugin?

No. Cesium runs without an install. It uses HTML5 standards, namely WebGL, that are natively supported by browsers.

What browsers are supported?

Recent versions of Chrome, Firefox, and Internet Explorer 11 are supported and tested with every release. Safari and Opera both work as well, but are not as actively tested.

What operating systems are supported?

Cesium runs on any platform with a WebGL capable browser, including Windows, Linux, OS X, Android, iOS, and Windows Phone.

What video cards are supported?

Cesium works on any video card that supports WebGL. This includes almost all NVIDIA and AMD cards made since 2004 as well as recent Intel video cards.

To see if your video card supports WebGL, visit our WebGL Report.
