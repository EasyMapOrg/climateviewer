Welcome to the new Climate Viewer!

Climate Viewer Mobile's live updates on severe weather, earthquakes, fires, and other life-threatening events will give you peace of mind in one all-inclusive app. With cutting-edge technology, real-time situational awareness, and a visual tour of our planetary health risks, look no further. We have you covered, spread the word! This website is under construction, please report any issues and have fun! Knowing is half the battle.
